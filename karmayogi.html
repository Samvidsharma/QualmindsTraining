Sloka 1

Nahikaschit kshanamapi jatu tishthatyakarmakrutu |
Karyatehyavasah karma sarvah prakruti jair guniha: ||

Sloka 2

Sadrushamchestate swasyah prakruteir gnana vanapi |
Prakrutimyanti bhootani nighraha kim karishyasi||

Sloka 3

Sreyan swadharmo vigunah paradharmath swanushtitaat |
swadharme nidhanam sreyaha paradharmo bhayavahaha: ||
