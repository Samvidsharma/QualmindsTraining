GUNAATITHA

In Gita 14th Chapter Krishna explained about GUNAATITHA

  1. prakashancha pravruttincha moham eva cha pandava
    na dwesti sampravruttani na nivruttani kamkshathi

  2. Udaaseenavadaseeno gunairyo na vichalyate|
    Guna Guneshu vartantam Yovatisthathi nengate

  3. "Sama duhkkha sukha swastah: sama lostashma kanchanah:|
    Tulya priyaapriyoo Dheeraha: Tulya nindatma samstuthi:|"

  4. manaapamanayoostulyo tulyo mitraaripakshayooh
    sarvaarambha parityagi GUNAATITHAsa uchyatee
