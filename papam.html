1. Kama Esha Krodha Esha Rajo Guna Samudhbhavah |
    mahashano mahapapma vidhyeham iha vairinam ||

  2. Dhumenavriyate vanhir yada darsho malenacha |
    Yatholbenaavrutho gnanaha: tadateeneda mavrutam ||

  3. Avrutam Gnanametena Gnaninoo nitya vairinaa |
    Kamarupena Kounteya dushpurenanalenacha ||

  4. IndriYani ManoBudhirasyadhistanamuichyate |
    Yatirvimohayatyesha gnanam avrutya dehinam ||

  5. Tasmaat tvam indriyanyadou niyamya bharatarshabha |
    Paapmaanam Prajayihyenam gnana vignana nashanam ||

  6. IndriYani paraanyahurindriyeebhah param manaha:|
    Manasastu para budhya yo budhe paratastu saha||

  7. Evam budheeh param budhva samstabhyatmanamatmanaa |
    Jahi shatrum maha bahoo kamarupam duraasadam ||
