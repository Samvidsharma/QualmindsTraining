OPAMP OPERATIONAL AMPLIFIER


OpAmp Definition

An operational amplifier is a DC-coupled high-gain electronic voltage amplifier with a differential input and, usually, a single-ended output

Unlike Other Amplifiers Opamp is used in Linear region and hence can be used to Design Circuits like:

  1. Summer
  2. Subtractor
  3. Multiplier
  4. Buffer
  5. Comparator
  6. Differentiator and Integrator
  7. Filters
    and etc

OpAmp is represented as


  1. Opamp As Summer and Subtractor
  2. Opamp as Integrator and differentiator
  3. Opamp as Comparator
